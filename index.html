About KOBE

Kobe was the first guard to be drafted by the NBA out of high school. Kobe played for the Los Angeles Lakers his entire professional career. He was the youngest player to start an NBA game. Kobe's mom's brother, John Cox, also played in the NBA.

Born: August 23, 1978, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States

Died: January 26, 2020, Calabasas, California, United States

Spouse: Vanessa Bryant (m. 2001–2020)

NBA draft: 1996 (Round: 1 / Pick: 13)

Dates joined: 2006 (Los Angeles Lakers), 1996 (Los Angeles Lakers), 1992 (Lower Merion Boy’s Basketball Team)

Gold medals: Basketball at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament
